This deal, brokered after a meeting between Ukrainian President Volodymyr Zelensky and Greek Prime Minister Kyriakos Mitsotakis, aims to help Ukraine get the energy it needs and help Greece increase the flow of American LNG to its terminals to "replace Russian gas in the region"
Why this matters: This deal highlights the ongoing shift in European energy reliance away from Russia following its invasion of Ukraine, indicating a growing dependence on US LNG for regional energy security.
