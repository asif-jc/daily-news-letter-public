Ars Technica • 27 Nov 2025, 6:19 AM NZDT • Priority: 9/10

Why this matters: This decision by HP is a clear indicator of the growing trend of businesses prioritizing AI investments over human capital to achieve cost savings and increased efficiency. BBC World News • 28 Nov 2025, 12:03 AM NZDT • Priority: 7/10

Businesses should be aware of how quickly international tensions can disrupt operations and consider diversifying their risk exposure.

Fracking has transformed an Argentine town - but what about the nation?
BBC World News • 27 Nov 2025, 1:02 PM NZDT • Priority: 6/10
The Argentine town of Añelo experienced significant growth following the start of fracking in 2014, with its population increasing by over 60% and attracting thousands of daily workers. This boom transformed the town from a sleepy rural area into a thriving energy hub, highlighted by the success of local businesses like Fabio Javier Jiménez's tyre repair shop.
Why this matters: Añelo's transformation showcases the potential economic benefits fracking can bring to specific regions, but it also raises questions about whether this localized prosperity can translate into national-level gains for Argentina. For business professionals, it highlights the opportunities (and potential challenges) of investing in areas undergoing rapid development driven by resource extraction, and the wider debate around the socio-economic impact of fracking.
