Why this matters: This agreement signifies a major escalation in economic pressure on Russia and signals a willingness by Ukraine's allies to endure potential energy price increases and disruptions to global supply chains. This could indicate a trend of EV companies prioritizing wider consumer appeal over niche, luxury offerings.
