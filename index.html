BBC World News • 30 Sep 2025, 3:06 AM NZDT • Priority: 9/10

This deal, understood to be the largest leveraged buyout ever, will take EA private and represents a 25% premium on the company's market value.
Why this matters: This acquisition highlights the continued consolidation and surging valuations within the gaming industry, with sovereign wealth funds and private equity increasingly looking to invest in valuable entertainment properties. It also raises potential questions about the influence of Saudi Arabia's PIF in the gaming landscape and the future direction of EA's popular franchises under new ownership. Finally, the comparison to the Microsoft/Activision Blizzard deal underscores the ongoing regulatory scrutiny surrounding major tech mergers, as they could potentially impact market competition.
